Common XDG directories on Mac:

```sh

$XDG_CONFIG_HOME: ~/.config
$XDG_DATA_HOME: ~/.local/share
$XDG_STATE_HOME: ~/.local/state
$XDG_CACHE_HOME: ~/.cache
```

For Neovim specifically:

Config: `~/.config/nvim`
Data/plugins: `~/.local/share/nvim`
Cache: `~/.cache/nvim`
State: `~/.local/state/nvim`
